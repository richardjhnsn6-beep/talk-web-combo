## 🔧 HOW IT WORKS (Technical Overview)

**The technology is called "RAG" (Retrieval Augmented Generation):**

### **Step 1: Storage**
- All your teachings stored in MongoDB database
- Each document tagged with topics (Hebrew, Bible History, Book of Amos, etc.)
- Converted into searchable "embeddings" (AI-readable format)

### **Step 2: Search**
- When someone asks a question, system searches the database
- Finds most relevant teachings (top 3-5 matches)
- Retrieves the exact text you wrote/said

### **Step 3: Response**
- AI Richard reads the retrieved knowledge
- Formulates an answer in your voice
- Includes citations ("Based on Richard Johnson's teaching in...")
- Maintains your authentic tone and perspective

### **Example:**

**User asks:** "What is YASHARAAL?"

**System:**
1. Searches knowledge base for "YASHARAAL"
