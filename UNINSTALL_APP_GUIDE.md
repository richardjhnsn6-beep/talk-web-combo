# 🔧 How to Uninstall RJHNSN12 App from Your Computer

If you accidentally installed the RJHNSN12 app on your desktop/laptop and want to remove it:

## Windows:

1. **Close** the RJHNSN12 app window (if open)
2. Go to **Settings** (Windows key + I)
3. Click **Apps** → **Installed Apps**
4. Search for "rjhnsn12" or scroll to find it
5. Click the **three dots (⋮)** next to it
6. Click **Uninstall**
7. Confirm

## Mac:

1. **Close** the RJHNSN12 app
2. Open **Finder** → **Applications**
3. Find "RJHNSN12" app
4. **Right-click** → **Move to Trash**
5. Empty Trash

## Chrome (Alternative):

1. Open Chrome
2. Go to: `chrome://apps`
3. Find RJHNSN12
4. Right-click → **Remove from Chrome**
